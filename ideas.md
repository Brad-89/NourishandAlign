## Chosen Direction

The chosen direction is **Contemporary Organic Editorial** because it best matches the requested warm and holistic vibe while still allowing the website to feel premium, trustworthy, and conversion-focused. This direction will guide the visual identity, copy rhythm, section architecture, CTA placement, and animation style throughout the website.

## Competitor Pattern Notes

The reviewed competitor sites suggest that visitors respond well to clear service categories, practitioner-led warmth, visible booking routes, testimonials, and careful explanation of how nutritional therapy, coaching, testing, and homeopathy fit together. The new website should improve on these examples by making the discovery call pathway more prominent, using stronger SEO structure, and presenting the three service areas as an integrated whole-person method rather than separate disconnected offerings.
